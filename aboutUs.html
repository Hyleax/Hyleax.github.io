About Us

Locked is a newly established Malaysian based company that is looking to BREAK into the shoe and sneaker market. The name “Locked” was derived from the various lockdown situations happening across the globe. As such, Locked is prioritizing pricing and affordability as its main selling points. Locked hopes to provide affordable, stylish, and quality footwear especially sneakers to many people across the globe.

Our philosophy
Locked uses the slogan “Buy it now, Wear it Later” to encourage customers that purchase Locked’s products to stay indoors and social distance and wait for the right time to wear their brand new shoes when the situation is safe and clear.
